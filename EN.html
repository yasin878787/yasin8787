How to Read Women's Body Language

Eye Contact

Frequent and Direct Eye Contact Shows Interest and Attention

Avoiding Eye Contact or Looking Down May Indicate Shyness or Discomfort

Brief Glances After Stealing a Look May Indicate Initial Interest or Temporary Attention

Facial Expressions

A Warm and Genuine Smile Shows Interest or Comfort, While a Fake Smile May Indicate Politeness or Formality

Raised Eyebrows: May Indicate Surprise or Curiosity

Lips Touching or Biting May Indicate Anxiety, Attraction, or Even Deception

Hand and Arm Movements

Open Arms and Palms Up Show Acceptance and Comfort

Crossing Arms or Holding Them Close May Indicate Self-Protection or Uncertainty (Try to Reassure Them, e.g., Share a Family Story)

Touching Face and Neck

May Indicate Anxiety or Interest

Body Posture

Leaning Towards You May Indicate Interest and Attention

Leaning Away or Moving Back May Indicate Disinterest or Discomfort

Crossing Legs and Arms

May Indicate Self-Centeredness or a Protective Feeling
